Help: How to share RSS on Twitter

1. Connect Twitter account

Click on "Sign In with Twitter" link or button to start.

Sign in with Twitter


Authorize rsstw.it application. We need permission to post tweets on your timeline.

You will be redirected back to the application.

2. Add RSS feed

Click on "Add RSS feed" button to create new RSS feed.

Enter RSS feed URL and choose Update Interval from 1 hour to 24 hours.

Now your feed is ready.

3. Check your tweets

To see the list of last tweets go to Report section.

New tweet is posted on your Twitter timeline.
